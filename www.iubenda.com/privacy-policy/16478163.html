Google User Data

What Google User Data Wabi may access
When you connect your Google Account to Wabi, we may access certain Google user data with your consent, through Google APIs (such as Gmail and Calendar). This data may include:
- Basic profile information (name, email address)
- Gmail data (subject lines, sender/recipient addresses, timestamps, message content)
- Calendar events (titles, start/end times, attendees)
You can review and manage the permissions granted at any time in your Google Account settings
or directly in the Wabi mobile app under Account Settings → Integrations, where you can immediately disconnect Gmail and Calendar integrations.

How We Use Google User Data
We use Google user data solely to provide and improve the functionality of Wabi apps, including:
- Displaying your Gmail data inside the Wabi interface to help you organize workflows.
- Reading calendar events to help schedule and synchronize app features.
We do not use Google user data for advertising, profiling, or unrelated analytics. We do not sell Google user data.

Sharing of Google User Data
We do not share Google user data with any third parties except:
- Service providers that help us securely store or process data (e.g., hosting and infrastructure providers), under strict data protection agreements; or
- When required by law, to comply with legal obligations.
We never share Google user data for advertising or marketing purposes.

Storage and Protection of Google User Data
Google user data is stored securely using encrypted storage and transmission protocols. Access is restricted to authorized personnel and services strictly necessary to operate Wabi. We apply industry-standard security measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss.

Retention and Deletion of Google User Data
We retain Google user data only for as long as necessary to provide the services you requested.
You can revoke Wabi’s access to your Google data at any time either through your Google Account permissions or directly in the Wabi mobile app under Account Settings → Integrations, where you can immediately disconnect Google integrations such as Gmail and Calendar.
You may also request deletion of any data stored by Wabi by emailing support@wabi.ai. We will delete the data within 30 days, except where retention is legally required.
